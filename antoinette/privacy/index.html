Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-19

This Privacy Policy explains how Antoinette (“Antoinette”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, and shares personal data when you use the Antoinette mobile app and related services (the “Service”).

We are the data controller for the purposes of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

  • Controller: Antoinette, TBD
  • Contact: social.antoinette@gmail.com

What Antoinette does

Antoinette is a personal coordination companion. You talk to it by voice or text about your plans, your friends, and how you are doing; it organises that into a daily brief, helps you stay in touch with friends, and surfaces shared plans and check-ins. To do this it processes the content you share, using third-party AI services for transcription and language understanding.

Data we collect

1. Account and identity data

Depending on how you sign in, we collect:

  • Sign in with Apple — an Apple user identifier, and the name and email you choose to share (Apple may relay a private email).
  • Google Sign-In — your email address and display name.
  • Email sign-in — your email address and a one-time verification code.
  • Telegram (if you link it) — your Telegram user ID, username, first/last name, and chat ID.

We also store a device identifier (a per-install ID derived from the OS vendor identifier, or a random value) to manage your sessions and devices.

2. Content you provide

  • Voice recordings you make in the chat composer, during onboarding, and in feedback. Audio is uploaded to our storage and sent to our transcription provider; the resulting transcript is stored.
  • Text messages and onboarding answers you write.
  • Photos you attach to messages.
  • Feedback you submit.
  • Profile data — display name, username, bio, and profile picture.

3. Friends and contacts

  • Friend connections you create and the access categories/permissions you set for them.
  • Contact matching (optional): if you use friend discovery, the app reads phone numbers and email addresses from your device address book and sends a truncated, normalised set to our server only to find friends already using Antoinette. We do not store your contact list; matching is transient.

4. Calendar and scheduling

If scheduling features are used, event and free/busy information is processed to detect conflicts and propose plans, and stored when you save an event. Antoinette reads from a shared service calendar via a service account; it does not read your device personal calendar.

5. Notifications and technical data

  • An Expo push notification token so we can send you notifications.
  • Basic technical metadata: platform (iOS/Android) and app version.

Permissions the app requests

  • Microphone — to record voice messages. Recording only happens when you start it.
  • Notifications — to send daily briefs, friend updates, and plan reminders.
  • Contacts (optional) — only when you use friend discovery, as described above.

You can change or revoke these in your device settings at any time.

How we use your data

  • To provide and operate the Service (store your messages, generate your daily brief, coordinate plans, connect you with friends).
  • To transcribe voice recordings and understand your messages using AI services.
  • To send notifications you have enabled.
  • To respond to feedback and support requests.
  • To secure the Service and prevent abuse.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Legal bases (GDPR)

  • Performance of a contract — to provide the features you request.
  • Consent — for microphone access, contact matching, and notifications; you can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legitimate interests — to secure and improve the Service.

AI processing

Your text, voice recordings, onboarding answers, and feedback are processed by OpenAI for transcription and language understanding. Content sent for AI processing is used to produce your results and is subject to OpenAI’s terms; we do not use your content to train our own models.

International transfers

Some providers (including OpenAI and our hosting) process data on servers outside the EU/EEA, including the United States. Where we transfer personal data internationally, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses.

Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. Voice recordings and their transcripts, messages, and photos are retained until you delete them or delete your account. When you request account deletion we delete or anonymise your personal data, except where we must retain it to meet legal obligations.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing of your personal data, and the right to data portability and to withdraw consent. To exercise any of these, contact social.antoinette@gmail.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Deleting your account and data

You can delete your account directly in the app under Settings → Account → Delete account. Your account is deactivated immediately and permanently deleted after 30 days (sign in again within that window to reactivate). If you no longer have the app, see the account deletion page or email social.antoinette@gmail.com.

Children

Antoinette is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact social.antoinette@gmail.com and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the new version here and update the “Last updated” date; significant changes will be communicated in the app.
