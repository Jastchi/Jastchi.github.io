Child Safety Standards

Last updated: 2026-06-20

Antoinette has a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). This page describes our standards and how to report violations.

Prohibited content and behaviour

The following are strictly prohibited on Antoinette:

  • Any content that sexually exploits or abuses minors (CSAM).
  • Grooming, solicitation, or any attempt to exploit children.
  • Sharing, requesting, or distributing content that sexualises minors in any way.
  • Using the Service to arrange or facilitate abuse of a child.

Age requirement

Antoinette is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly permit minors under 16 to create accounts. If we learn that a user is under 16, we will delete their account and associated data.

Reporting

If you encounter content or behaviour on Antoinette that violates these standards, please report it immediately by emailing social.antoinette@gmail.com.

We review every report and act on confirmed violations promptly.

Enforcement

Confirmed violations result in:

  1. Immediate account suspension.
  2. Permanent ban from the Service.
  3. Reporting to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and/or relevant local law enforcement as required by law.

We co-operate fully with law enforcement investigations.
